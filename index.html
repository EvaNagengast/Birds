What is a bird?

Ah, the enigmatic creatures known as birds. These feathered wonders are like nature's answer to a riddle we never knew we had. So, what exactly are birds? Well, imagine if you took a dinosaur, gave it a trendy makeover, and taught it to sing like a pop star. That's basically a bird!

Think of them as nature's answer to airborne superheroes. They're like the Avengers of the animal kingdom, rocking wings instead of capes and effortlessly gliding through the sky. Birds are the true pioneers of flight , they have lightweight bodies, hollow bones, and beaks that are as versatile as a Swiss army knife.

And let's not forget their knack for serenading us with enchanting melodies that can make any pop star green with envy. So, in a nutshell, birds are Mother Nature's vian wonders.
So, if you ever spot a creature with feathers, wings, and a knack for defying gravity, you've found yourself a bird!
